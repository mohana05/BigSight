About the App

BigSight is a comprehensive vision accessibility app designed for seniors and visually impaired users. It provides powerful magnification, text reading, document scanning, and safety features — all with an easy-to-use, large-button interface.

Key Features

  • Read Text — Magnify and read text with your camera
  • Read Aloud — Text-to-speech for any text
  • Document Scanner — Scan and save documents
  • Medication Scanner — Identify medications
  • Currency Identifier — Identify bills and denominations
  • Expiry Date Scanner — Check product expiry dates
  • Emergency & Medical ID — Quick access to emergency contacts
  • Fall Detection — Automatic fall alerts
  • Voice Commands — Hands-free operation
  • Battery Announcer & Talking Clock — Audio status updates

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my subscription on a new device?

Go to Settings → Subscription → Restore Purchases. Your subscription is linked to your Apple ID and will be restored automatically.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → BigSight → Cancel Subscription. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

The camera shows a white/blank screen

Close and reopen the app. Make sure you've granted camera permission in Settings → BigSight → Camera.

Notifications aren't working

Go to iPhone Settings → BigSight → Notifications → enable Allow Notifications and Time Sensitive Notifications.

Does the app work offline?

Yes! All features work offline. No internet connection is required for magnification, scanning, or text-to-speech.
