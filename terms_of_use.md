## 2. Description of Service

BigSight is a vision accessibility app that provides tools to help users read text, scan documents, identify objects, and access safety features. The App includes both free and premium subscription features.

### Key Features Include:
- Text magnification and reading assistance
- Live text recognition (Reading Lens)
- Document, medication, handwriting, barcode, bill, currency, and expiry date scanning
- Color identification
- Text-to-speech reading
- Fall detection and daily check-in
- Emergency contact management
- Medical ID storage
- Voice commands

## 3. Not a Medical Device

**IMPORTANT:** BigSight is **not** a medical device, and is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any medical condition.

- **Medication Scanner:** The medication scanning feature uses optical character recognition (OCR) to read text on labels. It may produce inaccurate results. Always verify medication information with your pharmacist or healthcare provider. Never rely solely on the App for medication decisions.
- **Expiry Date Scanner:** Expiry date detection is based on text recognition and may be inaccurate. Always visually confirm expiry dates when possible.
- **Fall Detection:** The fall detection feature uses device motion sensors and may not detect all falls, or may produce false alerts. It is not a substitute for professional medical alert systems.
- **Medical ID:** The Medical ID feature stores information you enter. We do not verify the accuracy of this information. Ensure your medical data is accurate and up to date.
- **Daily Check-in:** The daily check-in feature relies on local device notifications and SMS messaging. It is not a monitored safety system and should not be relied upon as the sole means of safety monitoring.
